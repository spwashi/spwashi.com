Component Options / Spacing

Density & Measure

Spacing scales smoothly when driven by density attributes rather than hard-coded inline values.

Density: dense

High information per inch. Pads tightly and condenses line-height. Great for data registers, lists of settings, or tightly packed chips.

Density: soft

The default cadence. Provides comfortable breathing room for prose and general article blocks. Balances text measure against frame width.

Density: roomy

Expansive bounds for hero blocks, orientation frames, and introductory material. Creates a moment of pause inside the scroll.

Reading: measure

Caps the width to roughly 65ch (characters) for ideal reading of long-form prose, avoiding eye fatigue on wide screens.
