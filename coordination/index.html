#>project_rule

Coordination Rule

Every project should produce one of four things:

  1. A public artifact.
  2. A useful record.
  3. A funded offer.
  4. A reusable method.

Attention To Coordination

  1. Publish an insight, ask, offer, or proposal.
  2. Turn it into a Local Proof Card.
  3. Screenshot it.
  4. Share it to TikTok, group chat, DMs, or notes.
  5. Link back to /now/ or /cards/.
  6. Convert interest into support, membership, commission, or collaboration.
  7. Archive the card as a reference.
