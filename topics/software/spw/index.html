Spw Alchemistry

Transformation Model

Alchemistry is the disciplined transformation of attentional material. Curiosity becomes a probe, relation becomes a reference, intention becomes action, action becomes stream, stream becomes object, and object becomes surface only when the change remains inspectable.

Brace Physics

A brace stores charge when attention dwells near a boundary. That charge may bias the next projection, but it must never hide alternatives or leave the page feeling inert after an unavailable click.

Prefix / Postfix

A prefix operator primes how the next structure should be read. A postfix operator marks residue: what the structure leaves behind after contact. They mirror each other without collapsing into the same timing.

Future UI Surface

These pages are static first. Later, Spw blocks and scripts can target the same operator contracts to become widgets, inspect panels, spell boards, or LSP-backed navigation surfaces.
