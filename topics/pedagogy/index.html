Learning Science / Pedagogy

Mindful Learning as Constructive Play

Spwashi.College is built on the belief that engineering depth is best achieved through mindful curiosity and constructive play. This stub outlines the pedagogical frameworks—from Papert’s constructionism to modern cognitivist models—that inform how I lead groups and build tools.

Foundational Models

Constructionism

Seymour Papert's theory that learning happens best when people are engaged in making tangible objects. The foundation of the "Workbench" philosophy.

wiki: Constructionism

Vygotsky's ZPD

The Zone of Proximal Development: the distance between what a learner can do alone and what they can do with guidance. Used to calibrate the "Staff Engineering" depth here.

wiki: ZPD
