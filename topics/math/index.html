Math / Structure

Math is the structure under the surface.

This route collects the mathematical neighbors of the site’s design and software work. Some of that math is geometric, like curved space, transforms, and projection. Some is discrete, like lattices, parsers, grammars, and scheduling. The point is not to force everything into one theory. The point is to keep the structural disciplines close enough that they can inform each other.
