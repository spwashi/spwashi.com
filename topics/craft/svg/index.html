SVG Storytelling

A motif can be a tiny machine for memory.

SVG is useful because a mark can stay crisp, reusable, and editable. The goal is not to replace an illustrator. The goal is to give an illustrator a small system to inhabit.

Photo References For Motif Systems

A photo reference is useful when it gives the motif family a silhouette, a fold, or a rhythm to simplify. The SVG does not need to imitate the image. It needs to translate what the image is doing structurally.

@illustrator_handoff

What To Ask An Illustrator For

Good handoff brief "Give me five marks that belong to the same story world: one cover glyph, two chapter dividers, one margin creature, and one map token. They should work as black silhouettes first, then accept site colors later."
