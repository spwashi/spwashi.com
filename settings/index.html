Notes

Why quiet by default?

The map and console are useful inspection tools, but they should not compete with the page. Quiet settings keep the instrument available without making every route feel like a dashboard.

What still activates frames?

Hash links, clicks, focus, hover, map selection, console controls, and mode switches still activate frames. The viewport setting only controls scroll-driven activation.

How do I recover hidden chrome?

Return to /settings/ from the header or direct URL and choose quiet or full display again.
