What I Do

Frontend systems

Architecture that begins with the reading layer and works downward. Pages that feel trustworthy because they announce their own state, remember their own history, and respond with intention rather than surprise.

Language & grammar tooling

Parsers, contracts, and visible structure. I make the invisible rules of a system legible — so they can be discussed, extended, and handed on without friction. Pretext.js is the clearest example: it reads prose and writes responsive physics.

Product architecture

Honest scoping, clear trade-offs, and decisions that survive quarters without documentation. I work at the staff level — where the conversation is about what to exclude as much as what to include.

Play & facilitation

RPG Wednesday is the clearest window into how I think with others. The table reveals everything: how someone holds ambiguity, defers to ideas, and improvises under gentle pressure. These same patterns shape documentation, onboarding, and interfaces.

How I Work

The work is public and social by design. The clearest way to know someone is to watch them think in public and play in public.

RPG Wednesday

A recurring tabletop session. Watching how someone runs or plays a game reveals how they handle uncertainty, credit others, and improvise together. This is the most honest context I can offer.

Pretext.js

A measurement-driven layout library. No reflow. Reads prose and writes CSS custom properties that let content breathe at any size. The code is the calling card.

spw-workbench

The public grammar research and systems thinking laboratory. Long-horizon work that becomes durable infrastructure. Visible by design.

This site

A living system: semantic HTML as ledger, CSS as material physics, JavaScript as readable state. Every component has a declared contract. The architecture is the portfolio.

Configure Your Engagement

Four quiet dimensions — time, scope, depth, tenure — resolve into a clear tier. The field is designed so the most reasonable choice feels natural, while deeper exploration reveals the full spectrum.

How the field works

Time weights toward Fast. Tenure toward Standard. Depth toward Premium. Scope amplifies every direction. The center resolves when one tier clearly leads. Below that threshold the right path is a conversation, not a formula.
