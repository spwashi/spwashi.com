Services

I craft systems that reveal their own architecture — pages that think, remember, and teach.

I build systems, not just surfaces. A modern website shouldn't break when a trending tool updates. Every project I deliver rests on three unbreakable layers: a reading layer that search engines and screen readers parse perfectly, a material layer of responsive physics, and a semantic contract layer that lets other minds trust the work.

I spent 15 years as a Software Engineer, leading teams and architecting systems with a compensation scaling from $150k to $184k/yr. What I Do

Interactive Book Covers

Combining illustration with responsive web physics. Traditional book covers are static; an interactive cover is a lightweight, performant canvas that responds to touch, scroll, and device orientation. Priced competitively to give independent authors a magical, memorable storefront presence.

Ebook Formatting & Typography

Semantically perfect, beautifully typeset digital books that flow flawlessly on any reader. This is where 15 years of learning to code pays off for authors: no broken chapters, no bloated markup, just pure, accessible reading layers engineered to professional publishing standards.

General Web Services

For independent business owners who need systems, not just websites. From custom web applications to robust landing pages, I build local-first frontend systems that announce their own state, remember their history, and never break unexpectedly.

Consulting & Systems Design

Honest scoping, clear trade-offs, and architectural decisions that survive quarters without documentation. Bringing staff-level rigor to smaller teams and ambitious independent projects, focusing on what to exclude as much as what to include.
