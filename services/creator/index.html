What I Do Myself

Cover Design

Covers that do the full weight of positioning the book: genre legibility, quiet type, and an image that survives thumbnail size. I draw before I lay out, which means the final cover has real art at its core rather than a stock assembly.

Typical turn: two to three release cycles (four to six weeks).

Illustration

Interior plates, chapter heads, ornamental initials, endpapers, character studies. If you have a manuscript and a feeling for its visual register, I can meet it. I work in digital ink and graphite, and I'm comfortable matching an existing series.

Priced per plate. Series discounts on request.

Ebook Formatting & Typography

Semantically clean EPUB and reflow-friendly digital typesetting. No bloated markup, no chapter-break weirdness — just careful HTML that reads correctly on any device, with typography that respects the prose.

Includes accessibility pass: reading order, alt text, language tagging.

Interactive Book Covers

A traditional cover is static. An interactive cover is a small, fast canvas that responds to touch, scroll, or device tilt — a storefront that feels alive. Priced competitively to give independent authors a memorable landing page.

Ships as a drop-in page you can host anywhere.
