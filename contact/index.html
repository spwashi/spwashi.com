Contact

The fastest way to get in contact with me is to use one of my services listed on /services, start from the current funding surface, or make the request concrete with a Local Proof Card. Otherwise, the best direct route right now is a DM on X, TikTok, or Instagram.

Course signups are currently once per season and include reasonable surfaces for visibility and discussion. Public surfaces like YouTube, software notes, and the blog are useful for context, but they are not the fastest path for outreach.

If you are arriving from the component glossary or RPG Wednesday, send a short DM with the route, the component or card lane, and whether you are thinking about screen-readable hooks, SVG cleanup, or JSON/video wizard surfaces. That keeps the design conversation concrete from the first message.
