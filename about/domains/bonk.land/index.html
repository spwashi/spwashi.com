Operational Role

Physics

Impulse event. Bonk changes the trajectory of whatever it touches. It is the crossing of fields — the moment boon and bane meet through bone and discharge.

In materials science terms: phase transition. An abrupt structural change triggered by accumulated energy reaching a threshold. Not gradual drift — a discontinuity. Before bonk: one state. After bonk: another. The transition itself is the event.

Spw operator: @action(...) — the imperative that triggers a transformation.

Valence

event — not a field but a moment. Bonk is what happens; the other four are what exists. Bonk is blessed by coincidence: the improbable contact that reveals hidden structure.

Humor is a form of physics. The unexpected trajectory change that still follows the rules — comedic timing is just bonk legibility.

In Practice

  • @design The unexpected interaction that reframes the whole interface. The affordance you discover accidentally that changes how you think about the product.

Crossings

  • bonk + bane The costly collision. Impact that triggers loss, forces a decision, or makes the constraint undeniable.
