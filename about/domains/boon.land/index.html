Operational Role

Physics

Attractive force. Boon pulls inward. It is what you move toward — the projected positive field.

In materials science terms: nucleation. A favorable site that attracts new structure, lowers the energy barrier for formation, and creates the conditions for growth. Boon doesn't push — it receives.

Spw operator: >surface — a rendered projection arriving from outside.

Valence

+1 — the first pole of the polarity axis. Defined by opposition to bane, carried by bone, discharged by bonk, transmitted by honk.

Boon is not always comfortable. A gift that costs nothing teaches nothing. The weight of the benefit is what makes it real.
