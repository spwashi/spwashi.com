Land Cluster

The land cluster is a small symbolic system. Boon and bane define the polarity, bone carries the structure, bonk marks the event, and honk sends the signal outward.

  • > boon +1 · arrival, benefit — the attractive field
  • ! bane −1 · departure, cost — the bounding constraint
  • ^ bone 0 · structural carrier — the neutral medium
  • @ bonk event · collision, state change — the impulse
  • ~ honk signal · outward relay, transmission — the wave
  • boonhonk.ing engine · genre hybridization and biome construction
  • factshift.com engine · physics of narrative transformation
  • factshift.center observatory · fact dynamics field station and atlas

Current Direction

I am aligning the public work around one durable thread: plain-text systems, richer syntax, interactive codeblocks, projection surfaces, LSP-aware navigation, and software that stays legible to the people using it. The clearest entry points are the software surface and spw-workbench.

That connects Spw, this site overhaul, the Obsidian application, and my interest in learning materials that keep reading, annotation, and reflection in one structure.
